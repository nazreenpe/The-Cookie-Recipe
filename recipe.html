Alison Roman's
Salted Butter Chocolate Chunk Shortbread Recipe

Yes we are talking about Alison Roman’s Salted Butter Chocolate Chunk Shortbread, or 'The Cookie'.
It is a cookie and a shortbread. And it has chocolate. What more can anyone ask for!
A very repeatable and flawless recipe. It tastes a-ma-zing!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons (2¼ sticks) cold salted butter, cut into ½-inch pieces
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup (packed) light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 6 ounces semisweet or bittersweet dark chocolate, chopped (but not too fine, you want chunks, not thin shards of chocolate)
  • 1 large egg, beaten to blend
  • Demerara sugar (for rolling)
  • Flaky sea salt
